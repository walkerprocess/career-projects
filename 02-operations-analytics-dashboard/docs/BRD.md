# Business Requirements Document

## Objective

Provide a lightweight analytics workflow that helps operations leadership prioritize logistics lanes for performance improvement.

## Stakeholders

- Operations manager
- Finance analyst
- Distribution center lead
- Carrier relationship manager

## Functional requirements

1. Load shipment-level data from a structured source.
2. Calculate lane and carrier KPIs.
3. Rank underperforming lanes by on-time rate and cost per unit.
4. Produce an executive-ready recommendation.

## Success metrics

- Reduce late shipments on worst lane by 15 percent.
- Lower cost per unit on targeted lane by 5 percent.
- Create a reusable weekly reporting process.
